My Cat Alphonse

Also known as my little demon

Here are some facts about Alphonse!

  • Alphonse was born in October 2020, during the height of the COVID-19 pandemic.
  • He was a stray and suffered from an eye infection that caused his left eye to be permanently scarred.
  • He was rescued by Tiny Paws Kitten Rescue in Stillwater, Oklahoma. His eye was treated and he was able to retain some vision.
  • He was adopted in February 2021 by Christine Jones.
  • He loves playing in tunnels and being outside on the balcony at the apartment he lives in.
  • In October 2021, he got a brother named Arturo. At first he wasn't very welcoming, but now they are best buds!
  • Alphonse loves to snuggle with his mom, and one of his favorite things in the world is a good chin scratch!

If you'd like to learn more information about the organization that rescued Alphonse, check out Tiny Paws Kitten Rescue.
